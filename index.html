Projects

Inaventuras

Inaventuras takes place in Alvorada do Porto, a fictional coastal city known for its historic seaport and near-daily rainfall. The story follows Max, a boy who embarks on thrilling adventures alongside Vanda, his best friend, and Ana, a newcomer to the city.

The plot begins when Max discovers an alien probe capable of creating hyper-realistic virtual realities. These simulations become the stage for discoveries, challenges, and dilemmas, where the protagonists explore complex worlds, meet remarkable characters, and learn lifelong lessons.

Development Status

I am currently organizing the script. You will be able to follow the story on my Fliptru page and other platforms. Stay tuned for updates!

About Me

I'm Felipe Alazari, I started drawing in mid-2020 and have been improving ever since. I enjoy drawing my own characters, developing stories, and working with web programming.

I've never worked on major projects or acted as a freelancer. Currently, my relationship with illustrations is just a hobby, but I plan to start in this field soon. I have the dream of publishing a comic book for the world, and I'm working to make this possible in the coming years.
